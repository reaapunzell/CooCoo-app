About Us

Founder: Thabo Mazibuko

Thabo Mazibuko, a farmer with a background in electronics, founded CooCoo to address the challenges faced by small-scale poultry farmers in South Africa. Inspired by the struggles of farmers to sustain operations, access affordable technology, and maintain animal welfare, CooCoo combines AI-driven technology with a commitment to empowering farmers. The goal is to provide smart feeding solutions that boost productivity, cut costs, and support sustainable farming, creating a brighter future for agriculture.
